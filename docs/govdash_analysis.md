# GovDash Deep Dive (Saved Analysis)

As of February 5, 2026.

## Company Snapshot
- GovDash positions itself as an AI platform to win and run government contracts, covering discovery through post-award execution.
- Y Combinator W22. Founded in 2021. Based in New York. Founders: Sean Doherty, Timothy Goltser, Curtis Mason.
- Announced a $30M Series B on January 15, 2026 led by Mucker Capital and BCI, with Northzone and Y Combinator participating. The company reported 16x revenue growth since Series A, 18x customer growth to nearly 200 companies, and expansion to Arlington, VA.

## Market Context
- Federal government contract expenditures were $774B in FY2024 (GSA FPDS report).
- GovDash expanded into SLED contracting and cites a $2T market for SLED, nearly 3x the size of federal prime contracting.

## Business Model (Observed)
- Enterprise SaaS with demo-led sales and custom pricing (no public pricing).
- Modular product suite encourages land-and-expand: 70% of teams use at least two modules; 73% run a proposal monthly.
- Enterprise security positioning including FedRAMP Moderate Equivalency for CUI-handling defense contractors.

## Offerings (Modules)
- Discover: opportunity discovery across federal and SLED sources.
- Capture: CRM-style pipeline, bid match, capability matrix, gate reviews, teaming partners, solution development, single opportunity record.
- Proposal: compliance matrices, annotated outlines, past performance matching, labor category alignment, Word assistant, submission tracking, graphics generation.
- Contract: past performance organization, CPARS workflows, deliverables tracking, monthly status reports.
- Dash (AI agent): summarizes solicitations, surfaces past performance, drafts capability statements, answers product/workflow questions.

## Data, Integrations, Workflow
- Opportunity sources: SAM.gov, GSA eBuy, PIEE, SLED portals (all 50 states).
- Workflow integrations: Word Assistant, SharePoint, Okta SSO, Salesforce, webhooks.
- Single opportunity record is the core data model across capture, proposal, and contract.
- Data Library for capability statements, resumes, technical documents, and boilerplate content.

## Traction (Company-Reported)
- Customers reported winning over $5B in government contracts in 2025.
- 501% YoY growth in Dash usage.
