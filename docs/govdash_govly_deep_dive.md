# GovDash + Govly Deep Dive (Saved Analysis)

As of February 5, 2026.

## GovDash - Product Footprint
- GovDash positions itself as an AI platform that spans the entire government contracting lifecycle.
- Core modules include Capture Cloud, Proposal Cloud, Contract Cloud, and the Dash AI agent.
- Proposal Cloud highlights compliance tooling, annotated outlines, content reuse, and proposal drafting workflows.
- Integrations include SharePoint, Salesforce, Okta SSO, and Word add-in workflows.

## GovDash - Workflow Highlights (Observed)
- Single opportunity record from discover to post-award.
- Compliance matrix + gap tracking during proposal.
- Post-award deliverables, CPARS prep, and status reporting.

## Govly - Product Footprint
- Govly emphasizes SLED (state/local/education) opportunity discovery with wide jurisdiction coverage.
- Market intelligence includes award data, budget insights, competitive intel, and buyer contact discovery.
- AI predictions and industry analysis reports are positioned as differentiators.

## Feature Implications for This Repo
- Saved searches + filters for federal and SLED opportunities.
- Market intelligence fields: contract vehicles, incumbents, buyer contacts, budget notes.
- Award intelligence records, competitor summaries, and predictive insights (planned).
- Pipeline customization, capture gating, and proposal drafting enhancements (ongoing).
