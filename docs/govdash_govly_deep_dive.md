# GovDash + Govly Deep Dive (Saved Analysis)

As of February 5, 2026.

## GovDash - Product Footprint (Observed)

### Capture Cloud
- GovDash describes a capabilities matrix that maps task areas to past performance
  to reveal gaps and supports gate reviews and teaming decisions. (support.govdash.com)
- Capture workflows highlight gate reviews as structured decision points. (support.govdash.com)

### Proposal Cloud
- Proposal Cloud includes a compliance matrix and “shreds” (compliance tagging of
  document text) with grouping and status tracking. (support.govdash.com)
- The proposal outline editor supports volumes and sections, drag-and-drop
  organization, page limits, and reviewer assignments. (support.govdash.com)
- Proposal Cloud includes document classification and redline/review workflows,
  as well as compliance package downloads. (support.govdash.com)
- Past performance and labor category tooling includes recommended key personnel
  and adjudication workflows. (support.govdash.com)

### Contract Cloud
- Contract Cloud positions post-award execution: past performance management,
  award data tracking, status reports, and CPARS workflows. (govdash.com)

### Dash + Integrations
- The integrations page highlights a Word Assistant for final edits with SmartArt
  graphics and built-in AI assistance. (govdash.com)
- Public integrations include SharePoint, Okta SSO, Salesforce, and webhooks. (govdash.com)

## Govly - Product Footprint (Observed)
- Govly positions itself as an AI platform with capture workflows, AI agents, and
  collaboration, offering enterprise plans and a free tier. (govly.com)
- Market intelligence includes budget documents, competitor wins, and automated
  research workflows. (govly.com)
- Coverage spans federal + SLED opportunities and 40+ contract vehicles. (govly.com)
- The platform highlights AI-powered RFQ predictions, award data, and teaming tools. (govly.com)
- Govly advertises CRM integrations such as Salesforce and Unanet. (govly.com)
- Govly’s API documentation references ingestion pipelines, email ingestion,
  procurement portal automation, and webhooks, with SOC II and CMMC claims. (docs.govly.com)
- The Govly awards page advertises AI-powered award summaries, award breakdowns,
  and contracting officer contact details. (govly.com)

## Business Model Signals (Inferred)
- Both GovDash and Govly emphasize demo-led sales and enterprise functionality
  rather than public pricing, suggesting enterprise SaaS motions. (govdash.com, govly.com)
- Govly’s public site mentions a free tier alongside enterprise offerings, implying
  a freemium or land-and-expand strategy. (govly.com)

## Build Implications for This Repo
- Capture: capability matrix, gate reviews, and structured bid decisions.
- Proposal: compliance matrix, shred tagging, outline editor, review mode, Word add-in.
- Post-award: CPARS prep, status reports, award intelligence.
- Market intelligence: award data, budget documents, competitor wins, contact discovery.
- Automation: AI summaries, predictions, and workflow agents.
- Integrations: Word Assistant, SharePoint, Salesforce/CRM, Okta/SSO, webhooks.
